Surviving Oregon's Seasons

You'll soon learn that mother nature is bi-polar here.

Oregon general tips:

  • Get snow supllies before you gert here, winter may not seem bad, but your neighbors will be annoyed if you constantly ask to borrow their things.
  • Our summers are Arizona's winters, so if you're from a warmer area, bring jackets for summer.
  • Be prepared to hear a lot about hunting and fishing.
  • Be prepared to hear about the creator of NIKE. Alot.
  • If you don't hike, you should try it! We have beautiful trails, and are well known for Smith Rock.
  • We have beautiful camping grounds that are a lot of fun to stay at, look into a few!
