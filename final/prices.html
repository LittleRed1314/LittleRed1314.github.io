Prices

All of our pricing is done but square footage of the areas of the house we will working on.

Log Washing

Log washing costs typically $750 to $1000. In order to get a rough estimate, we charge around $.35 per square foot.($1062 for $3000)

Chinking

Chinkning is priced based off a linear footage amount. The price per square foot can vary from job to job. Typically it costs between $5 to $10 per squarefoot with an average of $7.50 per square foot.

Complete Refinishing

A complete refinishings total varies from project to project as well based off of what products you want us to use. We typically charge about $94 per square footage.
