What We Do

Log Washing

We spray your house with a log washing soap, and then, using a power washer we rinse off the soap from your home. This will be a team of at least two(depending on the size of your house) and while one person is outside washing, the others will be inside ensuring that any leaks are taken care of!

Chinking

Log homes can cost owners thousands of dollars a year because of old chinking. Cold and and hot air leak out of the homes through these cracks and allow cold air and hot air from the outside to slip in. Our chinking service takes out the old chinking and replaces it with brand-new chinking. After chinking we then go over the chinking with a stain to match your home to give it more of a blended look!

Complete Refinishing
